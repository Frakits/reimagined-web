"Bamber And Davey"

"Bamber And Davey" Is A Modification Made For The Video Game Called "Friday Night Funkin'". It Is Filled With Imagination And Is Styled Based On Cartoony Feels You Watched On The TV As A Kid. The Plot Recalls An Unseparable Couple, Being Caught Into The Wrath Of A Protective Father, Having Transported Them Both Into An Alternate Universe Where They Meet New Foes. These Foes Being The Bamber And Davey. While Having Gone Through A Battle Using The Power Of Music. The Couple Has To Solve A Few Mysteries Lying Beneath The Surface. Will You Help Them Battle Against These Foes And Uncover Mysteries?

The Repository Hosted On Itch.io

"Freidae Nite S4m"

"Freidae Nite S4m" Is Another Modification Made For The Same Video Game Called "Friday Night Funkin'". It Presents An Original Character Based Off Of A Friend Of Ours, Who Is Going To Battle The Couple In The Outer Space On Unknown Lands.
The Modification Is Currently Not Hosted On Any Platforms.
